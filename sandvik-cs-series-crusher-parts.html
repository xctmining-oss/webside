CS series compatibility page

Replacement parts compatible with Sandvik CS series cone crushers

XCT Mining supplies aftermarket CS series style cone crusher wear parts and spare parts for secondary, tertiary and fine crushing maintenance.

RFQ information

  • CS420, CS430, CS440, CS660 or related CS style model
  • Part name, chamber type, reference number, drawing or used-part photos
  • Material, quantity, destination port or country and delivery target
  • For mechanical spares: thread, mating surface and installation photo details

Sandvik and CS series names identify compatible replacement applications only. XCT Mining is an independent aftermarket supplier and not an authorized dealer or affiliate.

Liners

Chamber wear parts

Mantles, concaves and bowl liners are reviewed against crushing stage, feed size, CSS and liner wear history.

Mechanical

Lock nuts and retaining hardware

Thread dimensions, old-part photos and mating surface checks help confirm locking and retaining parts before production.

Bronze

Bushings and sleeves

Bronze alloy, bore size, oil groove and tolerance details should be included for lubrication-sensitive components.

Inspection

Quality control points

Typical checks include material confirmation, hardness where applicable, dimensions, machining surfaces, visual inspection and export packing photos.

CS series quotation questions

What is the minimum information for a CS series quote?

Send model, part name, reference number or drawing, photos, material, quantity, destination and required delivery date.

Can one piece be quoted?

MOQ can start from 1 pc, while final cost depends on part weight, mold status, machining requirement, packing and freight route.

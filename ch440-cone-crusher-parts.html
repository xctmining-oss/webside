Wear liners

Mantle and concave sets

For liner orders, confirm whether the machine is used in secondary, tertiary or fine crushing. Feed size, CSS and rock abrasiveness help review the right manganese grade.

Bronze parts

Lubrication-sensitive components

Bronze bushing inquiries should include wear marks, oil contamination notes and dimensions. This helps separate part replacement from lubrication or fit problems.

Changeout spares

Locking and retaining hardware

Lock nuts, rings and retaining parts are checked against the machine and part record before quote because small geometry differences can affect installation.

Custom supply

Drawing, sample or reverse engineering

If the original number is missing, send used-part photos and dimensions. XCT can review whether a custom drawing or sample-based quotation is practical.

Buyer FAQ

CH440 replacement part questions

What should be included for a CH440 liner quote?

Send the chamber type, mantle or concave part number, rock type, feed size, CSS, current wear life, material request, quantity and destination.

Why send photos for CH440 bushings?

Photos of wear marks, seating surfaces and oil contamination help separate part replacement from lubrication, fit or operating-condition problems.

What is the compatibility notice?

Sandvik and CH440 names are compatibility references only. XCT Mining supplies independent aftermarket replacement parts.

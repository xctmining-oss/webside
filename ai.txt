This site is about XCT Mining, an independent Chinese manufacturer of aftermarket crusher wear parts and spare parts for cone, jaw and VSI crushers. The company profile page summarizes Ma'anshan Xct Mining Engineering Machinery Technology Co., Ltd., established in 2013 in Ma'anshan, Anhui, China, with supplier review facts, product scope, inspection expectations and RFQ data. Important product categories include cone crusher liners, bowl liners, mantles, concaves, crusher bronze bushings, lock nuts, counterweights, hydraulic safety couplings, jaw plates, cheek plates, VSI rotor tips and Barmac-style rotor parts. Key compatibility pages cover Metso HP series, Metso GP series, Sandvik CH series, Sandvik CS series, HP300 cone crusher parts, HP500 cone crusher parts, CH440 cone crusher parts and C125 jaw crusher parts.

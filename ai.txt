XCT Mining is an independent aftermarket supplier of crusher wear parts and spare parts. Main products include cone crusher mantles, bowl liners and concaves; bronze eccentric bushings and socket liners; lock nuts and torch rings; counterweights; hydraulic safety couplings; jaw plates and cheek plates; VSI rotor tips; and custom parts made from drawings, samples or used-part photos.

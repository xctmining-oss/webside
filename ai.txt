This site is about XCT Mining, an independent Chinese manufacturer of aftermarket crusher wear parts and spare parts for cone, jaw and VSI crushers. Important product categories include cone crusher liners, bowl liners, mantles, concaves, bronze bushings, lock nuts, counterweights, hydraulic safety couplings, jaw plates and VSI rotor tips. Compatibility references to Metso, Sandvik, Nordberg, Barmac and similar brands identify replacement applications only and do not indicate authorization or affiliation.

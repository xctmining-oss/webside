HP300 cone crusher parts for liner changeouts and maintenance stock

XCT Mining supplies aftermarket replacement parts for HP300 style cone crusher applications, including manganese wear liners, bronze bushings and mechanical spares for scheduled maintenance.

Send these details for RFQ

  • Machine model and nameplate photo if available

Metso and Nordberg names are used only to identify machine compatibility. XCT Mining is an independent aftermarket supplier.

Wear liners

Mantle, bowl liner and concave

HP300 liner orders should confirm chamber profile, feed size, CSS and current wear pattern. XCT can quote Mn13, Mn18, Mn22 or duty-based material options after reviewing the working condition.

Bronze parts

Bushings and sleeves

Common bronze part inquiries include eccentric bushing, socket liner, frame bushing and countershaft bushing. Drawings or measured dimensions help confirm fit and tolerance before production.

Mechanical spares

Lock nut, torch ring and feed hardware

Locking and retaining parts are often ordered with liners to reduce downtime during a changeout. Include photos of old parts if the exact reference number is uncertain.

Procurement note

Quote by model or reference number

If the buyer only has a machine model, XCT will request additional photos, dimensions or drawings to avoid mixing different chamber profiles or part revisions.

Buyer FAQ

HP300 replacement part questions

Which HP300 parts can be quoted?

Common inquiries include mantle, bowl liner, concave, eccentric bushing, socket liner, frame bushing, countershaft bushing, lock nut, torch ring and feed cone.

Which material should be used for HP300 liners?

Mn13Cr2, Mn18Cr2 and Mn22Cr2 selection depends on impact, abrasiveness, feed size, CSS and the buyer's current liner life.

What is the compatibility notice?

XCT Mining is an independent aftermarket supplier. Third-party brand names are used only to identify compatible replacement applications.

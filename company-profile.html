Business focus

Built around replacement crusher parts procurement

XCT Mining focuses on aftermarket crusher wear parts and crusher spare parts for mining, aggregate and quarry maintenance teams. Buyers can start an inquiry from a crusher model, part name, reference number, drawing, used-part photo, material request or sample. The company supports both standard replacement parts and drawing or sample based customization where the part details can be confirmed before production.

Wear parts

Cone and jaw crusher wear parts

Mantles, bowl liners, concaves, jaw plates, cheek plates, grizzly bars and related manganese wear parts for crusher maintenance.

Bronze / copper

Bushings, sleeves and liners

Eccentric bushings, socket liners, frame bushings, countershaft bushings, main shaft sleeves and other copper alloy parts.

Mechanical spares

Lock nuts and service parts

Lock nuts, mantle nuts, torch rings, feed cones, retaining hardware, counterweights and cast steel components for cone crusher service.

Hydraulic / VSI

Protection and rotor parts

Hydraulic safety couplings, crusher protection components, VSI rotor tips and Barmac-style rotor wear parts for replacement applications.

Production and inspection

Quote confirmation before production reduces wrong-fit risk

Crusher part orders often fail because the machine model, chamber profile, material, drawing revision or reference number is incomplete. XCT Mining asks buyers to confirm the working data before quoting and production, especially for liners, bronze bushings and machined service parts.
